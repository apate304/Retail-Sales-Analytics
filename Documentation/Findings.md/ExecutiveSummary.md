# Executive Summary

## Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data from a Superstore dataset to identify trends in sales, profitability, customer behavior, and regional performance. The analysis combines Excel, SQL, and Power BI to transform raw transactional data into actionable business insights.

---

## Business Objective

The objective of this project was to answer key business questions, including:

- Which product categories generate the most revenue and profit?
- Which customer segments contribute the most sales?
- Which regions perform best?
- How have sales changed over time?
- What opportunities exist to improve profitability?

---

  - Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

- SQLite

- Power BI
  - Interactive Dashboard Development
  - Data Visualization

---

## Dataset Summary

- Total Records: 9,994
- Unique Orders: 5,009
- Total Sales: $2,297,200.86
- Total Profit: $286,324.07
- Average Order Value: $458.61

---

## Major Findings

- Technology generated the highest sales and profit.
- Furniture generated strong sales but significantly lower profit margins.
- The West region produced the highest sales and profitability.
- Consumer customers generated the largest share of revenue and profit.
- Sales increased substantially between 2015 and 2017.
- Standard Class was the most frequently used shipping method.
- Furniture received the highest average discount, potentially impacting profitability.

---

## Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations are suggested:

1. Continue investing in high-performing Technology products.

2. Review Furniture pricing and discount strategies to improve profitability.

3. Expand successful sales strategies used in the West region to lower-performing regions.

4. Strengthen customer retention initiatives for high-value Consumer customers.

5. Continue monitoring yearly sales trends to support long-term forecasting and inventory planning.

---

## Conclusion

This project demonstrates an end-to-end data analytics workflow by combining Excel, SQL, and Power BI to transform raw retail sales data into meaningful business insights. The analysis highlights opportunities for revenue growth, profitability improvement, and strategic decision-making through data-driven recommendations.

# Retail Sales Analytics Project

## Business Problem

A retail company has experienced strong sales over the past several years, but management has noticed that profits are not increasing consistently across all products, categories, and regions.

The leadership team wants to better understand sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, and regional trends in order to make more informed business decisions.

As a Data Analyst, my objective is to analyze historical retail sales data using SQL, Excel, and Power BI to identify trends, measure key performance indicators (KPIs), and provide actionable recommendations that can improve profitability and operational efficiency.
## Project Objectives

- Clean and prepare the retail sales dataset.
- Explore the data using SQL.
- Calculate important business KPIs.
- Identify top-performing products and regions.
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
- Build an interactive Power BI dashboard.
- Present business insights and recommendations.
## Business Questions

1. What are total sales and total profit?
2. Which products generate the most revenue?
3. Which products generate the most profit?
4. Which regions perform the best?
5. Which categories are most profitable?
6. How do sales change over time?
7. Who are the highest spending customers?
8. What recommendations can improve business performance?
